Portrait Photography

Portrait photography, or portraiture, is the art of capturing a subject’s personality and character by focusing on their facial features and expression. Depending on the style employed, it can also show portions of the subject’s body and a beautiful background setting.

Portraiture is Present in Just About Every Aspect of our Society

Contrary to what most people believe, portraiture is not limited to the kind that you see exhibited in photo galleries. In fact, gone are the days when you have to be rich or famous to be a subject of portrait photography in the Philippines.

Portrait photography is the up-and-coming teen stars on the cover of the latest showbiz magazine, the well-dressed models on the billboards along the metropolitan highways, and the well-known politician in the front page of the broadsheet papers. On the other hand, it is also the happy family portrayed in an amusement park’s latest poster, the attractive product endorsers on the labels of your favorite canned good, and the glossy class photos you stashed away from your high school days.
